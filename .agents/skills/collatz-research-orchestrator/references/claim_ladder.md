# Claim Ladder

Use this escalation ladder.

## Level 0 — Advisory Only

Process guidance, setup notes, planning, or engineering.

## Level 1 — Computational Observation

Numerical samples, modular probes, exploratory factor patterns, plots, or performance notes.

## Level 2 — Reproducible finite computation

A script reproduces the result exactly for a specified finite level/model/slice.

## Level 3 — Verified finite identity

Exact symbolic witness exists and is checked independently or against canonical artifacts.

## Level 4 — Candidate finite theorem

Statement has all hypotheses and a proof sketch, but is not formalized.

## Level 5 — Formalized finite theorem

Lean or another proof assistant verifies the finite-level statement.

## Disallowed jump

No level on this ladder implies the global Collatz conjecture without a separate proof bridge.
