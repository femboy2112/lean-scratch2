==SECTION:: 05.rowsum.unit_full ==

Row-sum / all-ones eigenvector identity at r=3.

\[
S_{\mathrm{unit},r3}(t) \cdot \mathbf{1} \;=\; R_{\mathrm{unit},r3}(t) \cdot \mathbf{1}
\]

\[
S_{\mathrm{full},r3}(t) \cdot \mathbf{1} \;=\; R_{\mathrm{full},r3}(t) \cdot \mathbf{1}
\]

R_{unit,r3}(t) and R_{full,r3}(t) are the level-3 row-sum polynomials
emitted deterministically by the registered construction script.

[STATUS: Verified Fact]

==SECTION:: 05.perron.unit_full ==

Perron dominance / spectral-radius equality for real s > 0:

\[
\rho(B_{\mathrm{unit},r3}(s)) \;=\; c_3 \cdot R_{\mathrm{unit},r3}(t)
\]

\[
\rho(B_{\mathrm{full},r3}(s)) \;=\; c_3 \cdot R_{\mathrm{full},r3}(t)
\]

  domain         : real s > 0
  positivity     : B_{unit,r3} and B_{full,r3} are strictly positive,
                   so Perron-Frobenius applies
  equality_basis : combined with the row-sum identity, all-ones is the
                   Perron eigenvector

==SECTION:: 05.generic.det_rank_kernel ==

Generic determinant / rank / kernel over Q(t).

\[
\det\!\bigl(S_{\mathrm{unit},r3}(t)\bigr) \;\not\equiv\; 0
\qquad
\det\!\bigl(S_{\mathrm{full},r3}(t)\bigr) \;\not\equiv\; 0
\]

\[
\operatorname{rank}_{\mathbb{Q}(t)} S_{\mathrm{unit},r3} \;=\; 18
\qquad
\operatorname{rank}_{\mathbb{Q}(t)} S_{\mathrm{full},r3} \;=\; 27
\]

\[
\ker_{\mathbb{Q}(t)} S_{\mathrm{unit},r3} \;=\; \{0\}
\qquad
\ker_{\mathbb{Q}(t)} S_{\mathrm{full},r3} \;=\; \{0\}
\]

The same generic rank / kernel hold at the B-level because
B = c_3 * S(t) and c_3 != 0.

Certificates:
  exact finite-field rank certificates at:
    (p, alpha) = (11, 2)
    (p, alpha) = (13, 2)
  Each yields rank 18 (unit) and rank 27 (full).

  Certificate method establishes that the determinant polynomials are
  not identically zero in Z[t]. It establishes generic full rank over
  Q(t). It does NOT establish all-real-s determinant nonvanishing.
